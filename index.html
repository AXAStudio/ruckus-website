05 — Reading the numbers

What the stats mean

OPR

Offensive Power Rating — the points a team is estimated to contribute on its own, solved out of alliance scores across an event. It is comparable within a season but never between them, since each year's game scores differently.

Rank

Seeding after qualification matches. Rank 1 means the team enters the elimination bracket as an alliance captain with first pick of the field.

W-L records

Qualification-match results, which is what the public record exposes per event. Elimination-bracket results are reflected in the Winner and Finalist awards instead.

The award ladder

Qualifiers feed Super Qualifiers, which feed the NYC Championship, which sends its Inspire winner and top alliances to the FIRST World Championship in Houston.
